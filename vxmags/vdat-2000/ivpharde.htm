by Cicatrix
[August 1997]

How did you start out in computers?

    I started when I was about 12 old. my first computer is XT 8086 with CGA.

How and when did you start out in the virus scene?

    I got a copy of some viruses (dark avenger, denzuko, brain, and liberty) from my father and was interested about the cool payload from denzuko and liberty. but I don't have too much about computer viruses in that period. bla..bla.. and the last I found the sayha watpu and die hard viruses from my disk.bla..bla
    I don't remember when I started my first virus...right now I have created 20 viruses (maybe).

Have you written viruses? If so which would you like to take credit for?

    yup! I don't remember my virus names..hmmmm...bla..bla...Word.Phardera: my word macro viruses...Vesselin Bontchev make some credit for Word.Phardera in his paper (thanks VB). Phardera.Ita: this 'real' virus was written using Borland Pascal 7.0. And my best virus is Phardera.5824. This virus uses polymorphism, stealth, and a lot of antidebugging tricks. This virus is found in the wild.
