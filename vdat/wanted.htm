As many of you will have noticed I have decided to be less generous with the supply of VDAT. VDAT 2.000.0 was the last PUBLIC release, any subsequent releases will limited to insiders only. As of this release the access to the ZIP file will be "per request" only. Passing the specific URL around will not help since VDAT will be "secured" with a key file. Specific "insiders" will be granted access and a key file to VDAT updates. This key file will change with every subsequent release of VDAT. I know this key file might even become a trading item and there is no way I can prevent that. When and if I think all of this is gettig too much of a hassle I will stop releasing VDAT all together.

For those with access to VDAT and with access to something from the list other "trades" can be arranged. For more information about donating material contact me at cicatrix@worldmail.nl or vdat@crosswinds.net. For details on future VDAT releases check my http://vdat.cjb.net URL redirection.

I know this might sound contrary to what people are used from me, I used to support the "unlimited and free information" view with most parts of my computer virus collection. But look at it from another perspective, I could have stopped making VDAT all together, at least I'm leaving an "in" to those that want it.

Reactions to this "limited access" to VDAT have been mixed. I have had positive, negative, supportive, pleading and disappointed reactions and I must say I understood most of them. Some of them asked me to reconsider my decision and I did, but for now I have come to the same conclusion. That is not to say that my view on all this will never change, only the future will tell.
